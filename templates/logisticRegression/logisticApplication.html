Problem Description

The objective of this project is to classify whether a FIFA 24 player is Elite or Average based on their in-game statistics. A player is considered Elite if their Overall rating is 80 or above.

Dataset Explanation

The dataset used comes from Kaggle and contains information about FIFA 24 players, including their pace, shooting, passing, dribbling, defending, and physicality ratings, among other attributes. It contains over 17,000 unique player entries.

Data Preparation

The dataset was processed by selecting the relevant numeric columns (Pace, Shooting, Passing, Dribbling). A binary target variable was created: Elite = 1 if Overall ≥ 80, otherwise 0. The data was split into 80% training and 20% testing sets, and scaled using StandardScaler before training.
